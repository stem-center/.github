# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

At STEM Center, we are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and respectful environment. 

We aim to create a community where everyone — regardless of background, identity, experience level, or role — feels safe, valued, and empowered to contribute.

We expect all contributors, partners, and collaborators to uphold the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and respect.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating respect and empathy toward others
- Being constructive in feedback and discussions
- Accepting responsibility and learning from mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the community and organization
- Encouraging inclusive and diverse perspectives

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing sensitive information without authorization
- Any conduct that compromises security, ethics, or integrity

---

## Professional Conduct

All contributors must:

- Follow ethical and legal standards
- Protect confidential and sensitive data
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Act in alignment with STEM Center’s mission and values

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate these standards.

---

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it confidentially:

📩 it@stem.cv

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion and fairness.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Violations may result in:

- Warning
- Temporary suspension of contribution privileges
- Permanent removal from collaboration spaces

The severity of the response will be proportional to the violation.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All STEM Center repositories
- Communication channels
- Events, training sessions, and collaborative initiatives
- Any space where STEM Center is officially represented

---

## Our Values

We are guided by:

- Innovation with Excellence  
- Ethics and Integrity  
- Collaboration and Inclusion  
- Safety and Sustainability  
- Empowerment  

---

By participating in STEM Center projects, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.

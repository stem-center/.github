# STEM Center

Founded in Cabo Verde in 2017, STEM Center is a technology and innovation hub specialized in Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, and Digital Transformation.

We were created to address a critical gap in the market for data-driven solutions, empowering organizations and citizens to make intelligent, assertive decisions and drive sustainable growth.

---

## 🌍 Vision

To be globally recognized as a trusted partner in Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence, delivering innovative and impactful solutions that accelerate digital transformation.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Empower organizations to adopt a data-driven and AI-powered culture, enabling smarter decision-making, operational excellence, and long-term sustainable value creation.
